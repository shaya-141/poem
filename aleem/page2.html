Nature's Balance

As the seasons turn, the earth takes on new colors. The green leaves dance with the sunlight. The earth provides for its children. As the seasons turn, so too must we remember our purpose.

The seeds lie dormant in the dark, earthy womb. When the time is right, they will break free, To sprout and grow into the magnificent beings they were always meant to be. So too must we embrace our growth, for we are nature's children.
