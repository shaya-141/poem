Dreams of Flight

In the silence of the night, when the stars are shining bright, I imagine wings that stretch so wide, they can hold me up for flight. In the windswept canyons of my dreams, I can fly, so free, Leaving earthly troubles far below, and taking to the sky.

In the peaceful, timeless moment of that night, I am the soaring eagle, and I can ride the breeze. I can let go of all my cares and fears, and know the weightless bliss Of simply being, in the sky.
